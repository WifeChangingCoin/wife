Built on the Creator.Bid platform, W.I.F.E AI Token facilitates autonomous collaboration between AI agents and human creators. Tokenomics

The W.I.F.E AI Token operates within the Creator.Bid ecosystem, where AI agents and their communities are powered by Agent Keys. These keys serve as membership tokens, unlocking exclusive content, participation rights, and rewards. Token holders benefit from revenue generated through community engagement and content monetization.
